I grew up in small town Georgia where I went to college and studied classical music performance and Spanish. I’ve worked in the service industry for about eight years. I deeply appreciate the skills I learned as a server. I gained exceptional customer service skills. I sharpened my social skills, as I was constantly required to talk to new people daily. When I worked large parties, I learned how to command the attention of large groups of people while presenting them with information about the menu and daily specials. I moved to Kansas City in 2019 and decided that it was time for a change in career. My lifelong love for computers and led me to Centriq Training to study full stack web development. There, I completed 455 in class hours working mainly with Microsoft’s .Net framework. Later I moved to Washington D.C. and completed another 480 in class hours at General Assembly’s Software Engineering immersion class. I can’t wait to find a team of good people to collaborate with and grow my skills as a developer.
